How to Delete Your Data

DigiJar stores all your data locally on your device. You have full control over your data and can delete it at any time directly from within the app.

  1. Open DigiJar Launch the DigiJar app on your Android device
  2. Go to Settings Tap the gear icon (⚙️) in the top right corner of the home screen
  3. Find "Clear All Data" Scroll down to the "Data & Storage" section
  4. Confirm Deletion Tap "Clear All Data" and confirm when prompted

⚠️ Important

This action is permanent and cannot be undone. All your savings history, achievements, streaks, and preferences will be permanently deleted.

Data Retention

DigiJar does not retain any of your personal data after deletion. Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • • Data is deleted immediately when you use "Clear All Data"
  • • No backup copies are kept on external servers
  • • We have no access to your data at any time
  • • Uninstalling the app also removes all local data

Third-Party Data

If you consented to personalized ads, Google AdMob may have collected some advertising data. To manage this:

  • • Visit Google Account Settings to manage your ad preferences
  • • You can opt out of personalized ads in DigiJar's consent settings
